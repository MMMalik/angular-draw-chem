Manual

General

  • Open editor by clicking 'Open editor' button.
  • Close editor by clicking 'Actions/close' or by clicking ctrl + q. The structure you are working on won't be lost as long as you stay on this page and don't refresh.
  • Export your structure as svg by clicking 'transfer' (Actions/transfer) or ctrl + t (note: this shortcut is not working under Chrome, since Chrome's default binding for ctrl + t (tab opening) cannot be overriden). Close the editor to see that the result is shown on the webpage.
  • adChem is more responsive and faster under Chrome.

Bond drawing

  • Choose a bond from quick menu or from Structures. 'Single', 'double', 'triple', 'wedge', 'dash' and 'undefined' bonds are available.
  • Click on the drawing area.
    • If you click on empty space, a bond in 'south' direction will appear.
    • If you click on an atom, adChem will try to guess a direction of the bond to attach.

    Alternatively, keep left mouse button pushed on the drawing area and move cursor around to see where new bond will appear. If you keep ctrl key pressed while moving the cursor, a bond of custom length will be formed.

  • You may change the bond type at any time by choosing a new bond and clicking on the existing bond. E.g. if you want to change 'single' bond to 'wedge', just choose 'wedge' bond and click on a 'single' bond. 'Double' bond comes in three variations: centered, pushed to left, and pushed to right. To change between them, choose 'double' bond and click multiple times on a bond. You may quickly change multiplicity of a bond, by clicking on it (with 'single' bond chosen). E.g. draw a 'single' bond, click on it again and it will change to 'double', and again to make it a 'triple' bond. Click again to return to a 'single' bond. 'Wedge' and 'dash' bonds have two modes: normal and inverted. E.g. choose a 'dash' bond, draw it, then click on it once again.

Cyclic structures

  • Choose a cyclic structure from quick menu or from Structures. Currently, structures from three- to nine-membered rings are available.
  • Rules for drawing are similiar to those already described for bonds. Choose a structure, then draw it on the drawing area. You may keep left mouse button pushed on the drawing area and move cursor around to see where the cyclic structure will appear (the rotation is by 15 degrees). If you keep ctrl key pressed while moving the cursor, the structure will be rotated by custom angle. Currently, drawing of fused rings is not well-supported, but it will be soon :-).
